Know about our history

On December 12, 2002, the first Juan Valdez® Café store opened its door at the El Dorado international airport in Bogotá, with a dream story and great achievements for Colombian premium coffee.

The first international store was opened in the city of Washington D.C. We are currently in New York and continue to strongly develop our presence in and aorund Miami.
