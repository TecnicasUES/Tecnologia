know about our history

On December 12 2002, the first Juan Valdez® Café store opened its doors at El Dorado International Airport in Bogota. This was the beginning of a process that intended to introduce more and more people to premium Colombian coffee.


We opened our first international store in Washington D.C. in 2004. Today we are also in New York and Miami, where we are actively expanding our presence. We are continuing with the process of international expansion.
