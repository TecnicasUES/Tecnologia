Know about our history

On december 12, 2002 the first Juan Valdez® cafe store opened its doors at the El Dorado international airport in Bogota, with a dream story and great achievement for colombian premium coffee.


The first international store was opened in the city of Washington D.C. We are currently in New York and continue to strongly develop our presence in and around Miami.
